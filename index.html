About

I study electronics-free soft robotic systems that exploit nonlinear fluid–structure interactions to embed sensing, decision-making, and collective behaviors in the body and environment.

Interests

I’m interested in how nonlinear mechanics and fluidic interactions can create robust, autonomous behaviors without electronic sensing or computation.

Current topics include self-oscillating pneumatic actuators, interaction-driven locomotion, synchronization, and collective behaviors in soft robot swarms.
